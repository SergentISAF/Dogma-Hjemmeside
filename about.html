About Dogma

Twenty plus years at the bench.

Dogma was built on over twenty years of cable-building. What started as a hi-fi enthusiast's curiosity became an obsession, and is today a craft practised with a precision refined cable by cable.

To us, a good cable is not a luxury. It is the link that either lets a system perform at its best or holds it back. We have seen too many fine systems robbed of their potential because the cables between them were the first thing skimped on.

Every cable that leaves the Dogma workshop is cut, assembled, and soldered by hand. No assembly line, no outsourcing. When a cable leaves Dogma, it has been in one person's hands from the first to the last metre.

You don't buy a Ferrari and put cheap wheels on it. — Dogma
