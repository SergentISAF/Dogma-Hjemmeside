About Hans

Twenty plus years at the bench.

Hans Lauridsen has been building cables for over twenty years. It started as a hi-fi enthusiast's curiosity, became an obsession, and is today a craft he practices with a precision refined cable by cable.

To Hans, a good cable is not a luxury. It is the link that either lets a system perform at its best or holds it back. He has seen too many fine systems robbed of their potential because the cables between them were the first thing skimped on.

Every cable that leaves the Dogma workshop is cut, assembled, and soldered by Hans himself. No assembly line, no outsourcing. When you receive a cable from Dogma, it has been in one person's hands from the first to the last metre.

You don't buy a Ferrari and put cheap wheels on it. — Hans Lauridsen
