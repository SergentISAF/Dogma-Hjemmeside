Each cable made to order.

    Custom lengths. Choice of conductors and dielectrics matched to your system and listening room.

    RCA and XLR. Built to the right impedance and shielding, terminated to your wishes.

  • Power Cables

    High-grade mains leads for preamps, DACs, and power amps. Often the most overlooked link in the chain.

  • Special Builds

    An old termination, a particular geometry, or something off-the-shelf cannot deliver. Let's talk it through.
